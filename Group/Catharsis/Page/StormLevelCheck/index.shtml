I had to find out if there was a clipping problem with the piano part in Storm. I'm happy to report there is none - as you can see from above, the piano was recorded with decent headroom at a good level. The earlier clipping was from the mix of that recording and the PianoTeq "rhodes" being mixed too hot. I've dropped it down a bit and generated the above.

Interestingly, there is at least one missing note in the MIDI above. It would be easy enough to add back in from the context.
