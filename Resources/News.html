Click! - Improved!

Clicking is working better now. When clicking in the video area:
  • Intial click → play from the beginning
  • Single click while playing → move to that location
  • Click and hold (>1/2 second) → pause at current play location
  • Double click while playing → pause at click location
  • Click while paused → continue from paused location
In addition, keyboard events ("space" for Play/Pause, etc.) are working again. For more info, click here.

Time/Location Tagging

Time and Location Tagging is now working. Simply add a time string (e.g., :21, 1:23.4) or a locator number with a '#' (e.g., #1, #10) and it will turn into a link.
