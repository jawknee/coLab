Some highlights:

  • Recent Updates
    The top of the left side bar shows the most recent activity. The most recent is at the bottom of the list. Pages marked New! are "new" (currently less than two weeks old) and have not been commented on. Those marked (u) have been recently commented on (updated).
  • Current Projects
    Below that are the current projects - presently these are songs - project support is planned. You can link directly to a song, or part of one by clicking there.
  • Links
    Each content page is linked in a chain to all the other pages. These are currently chronological by creation date.
  • Archive
    Beyond that - the most useful item is probably the Archive link in the top nav bar. The Archive page will show every page, in chronological order, most recent first.

Hope that helps.
