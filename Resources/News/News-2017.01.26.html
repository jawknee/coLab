Click! Improvements

January 26, 2017
The click feature is now working in a more consistent and, I hope, more intuitive way.

The basic behavior is this:

  • Intial click → play from the beginning
  • Single click while playing → move to that location, continue playing
  • Click and hold (>1/2 second) → pause at current play location of initial click
  • Double click while playing → pause at click location
  • Click while paused → continue from paused location

In general, the changes are that when paused, a click will continue from the paused location, rather than jumping to the mouse location - particularly helpful when following links in email messages and such. Click-and-hold (for 1/2 second) will pause at the current play location when the mouse is first clicked (1/2 second before it stops, due to the delay). A double-click when playing will pause at the location of the click.

Hopefully this will make the use a bit easier. In addition, you can use "space" to toggle Play/Pause and the number keys 1-9,0 for locators 1-10.
